Season 1: 8-bit

8-bit games are video games with jagged 8-bit graphics and colors as minimally as possible. They are forced to choose blocky graphic style due to hardware limitations. This concept mainly describes the capabilities of the processor chip central to machines like the Nintendo Entertainment System and the Atari 7800 35 years ago. The development of society led to the birth of many different games and most of them were modernized to suit nowadays. The graphics to the characters become more and more realistic to everyone, gradually the games become more and more real The past has been lost and faded along with those developments. So, POC through this set of products is reminiscent of gamers about the 8 BIT games that have been storming for a while.
